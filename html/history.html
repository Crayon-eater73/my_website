The History Of What The Items Are And My Experiences

Item Box

The item box wasn't too difficult. The shading and textures were a bit difficult to replicate or improve.

Arm Flask

Building this out was very easy. However, I had major issues with texture painting, which forced me to spend most of the time figuring out how to set the object up for texture paint and then learning how to paint with a mouse.

Umbrella Corporation

This was one of the easiest parts of the project. I had no issues building it out and it was very easy to color.

Goop Juice

This was a little more difficult to make. I was unable to get the object to look like glass, but I managed and used basic colors.

How the items were built

First, I started with the base blocks, getting an idea of what my project was going to look like. Then I slowly added more detail to the blocks. I added smaller details like ridges and bumps. Finally, I went into texture paint and added more details onto the objects. Overall, it took a few weeks to troubleshoot and finish the models.
